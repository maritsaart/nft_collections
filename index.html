Proof of Work JPGs*

Collection created in collaboration with backend developer @aatabaaa.
The project took about three years to complete.

How on-chain NFTs became the answer to the reproach "NFTs are just URLs on the blockchain", similarly PoW JPGs have become the answer to the taunt "NFTs are just jpegs".

Each NFT from this collection has:
  • [1] hexspeak image SHA-256 hash (R8 register),
  • [2] hexspeak image IPFS CID v0 (R9 register - link to the artwork),
  • [3] hexspeak token Id,
  • [4] hexspeak minting transaction Id,
  • royalty by EIP-0024 V1 artwork standard,
  • 11 separator (analogue of ||) between the readable part and the rest of the hash.
All these four hashes of each NFT are mined on specially designed miners.

A Brief History regarding hash [3] (token Id mining).
The idea of the Proof-of-Work backed tokens was proposed in May 2020,
was first issued in September 2020 (+blog) and was implemented
as a service for Ergo community in October 2022 (but without royalties).

More information you can find at Proof of Work JPGs IPFS site.
